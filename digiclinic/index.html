What is the ECS?

The Endocannabinoid System is a body-wide receptor network regulating pain, sleep, stress, appetite, immune tone, gut motility, and reproductive balance via CB1 (brain/nerves), CB2 (immune), and endogenous ligands anandamide and 2-AG.

CB1 · CB2 · TRPV1 · 5-HT1A

CB1: brain, spinal cord — pain, mood, sleep, appetite.
CB2: immune cells, macrophages — inflammation, tumour microenvironment.
TRPV1: pain/heat desensitisation.
5-HT1A: anxiolysis, mood.

Aarohana™ Titration Protocol

Night 1 (mandatory): HALF target dose (X/2) — nocte only.
Week 1–2: Continue X/2, assess tolerance.
Week 3+: Advance to full X dose, divided BID.

Why Full-Spectrum > Isolate

Whole-plant Cannabis sativa Linn. includes CBD, THC, CBG, CBN, terpenes (β-caryophyllene CB2-selective, linalool GABA-A, myrcene CB1 potentiator) and cannflavin A/B (30× more potent than aspirin for COX-2). Entourage effect = broader efficacy at lower doses.
