Clinical guardrails

Scientific and logical use boundaries

This physician-facing file keeps the evidence useful without overstating indication claims or treatment efficacy.

Property ≠ disease claim

Mechanistic properties such as anti-inflammatory or neuroprotective must not be converted into product-level disease-cure claims.

Oncology boundary

Oncology rows remain research navigation. Cancer treatment decisions remain with the treating oncologist/RMP.

Clinical symptom support

Pain, sleep, appetite, CINV and QOL decisions require physician-led selection, dosing, monitoring and local regulatory compliance.
